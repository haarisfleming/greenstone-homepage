    Frequently Asked Questions

    Where do you deliver weed?

    We mainly deliver weed in California City. Aside from that, we also deliver in Adelanto, Apple Valley, Barstow, Hesperia, Victorville, Bakersfield, Boron, Lake Isabella, Lancaster/Palmdale, Mojave, Ridgecrest, Rosamond, Santa Clarita, and Tehachapi.

    Check here for a more detailed map of our delivery areas.

    What are your delivery hours?

    We do SAME-DAY deliveries for all orders placed before 12 pm. If you order after 12 pm, we will deliver the next day.

    How do I pay for cannabis delivery?

    You can pay in cash upon delivery.

    How long will it take for my cannabis delivery?

    Again, if you place your order before 12 pm, we will deliver your cannabis the same day. Depending on location, we try to reach your place no more than 2 hours after confirming your order.

    Is cannabis delivery free?

    Yes, the cannabis delivery is totally free but we follow a minimum-order rule to avail of our delivery service.

    Your order must be at least $50 if youre from Adelanto, Apple Valley, Barstow, Hesperia, Victorville, Bakersfield, Lake Isabella and its surrounding, Lancaster/Palmdale, Ridgecrest, and Santa Clarita and its Surrounding Area.

    You must place at least $40 if youre from Boron and Rosamond. If youre from Tehachapi, you need to place at least $45. For Mojave residents, place at least $35 worth of orders. And if youre from California, you only need to order at least $25 of cannabis.
